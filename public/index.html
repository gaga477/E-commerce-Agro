About Us

Helen Motherland Agro Export is a Nigerian food export company dedicated to bringing the finest authentic Nigerian food products to customers worldwide. From the rich soils of Nigeria, we source, process, and deliver premium quality food products to your doorstep — locally and internationally.

Our mission is to connect the world with the rich flavours of Nigerian cuisine, ensuring every product meets the highest standards of quality, hygiene, and authenticity.
